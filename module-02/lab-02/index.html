Jacksonville Florida. Home of the Jaguars!

About this map

Map Authored by Kevin Hogue in 2016 for New Maps Plus course Map672

According to the 2010 US Census, Jacksonville in the largest city in Florida. Jacksonville was named for Andrew Jackson who was the first military govenor of the Florida Territory and later went on to become the seventh President of the United States. In 1993, the NFL awarded Jacksonville an expansion franchise called the Jacksonville Jaguars and the city hosted the Superbowl XXXIX in 2005.
